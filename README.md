# ClickMine

ClickMine is an incremental crafting and automation game where you mine ores, smelt ingots and produce armour and weapons to sell for profit.

Gather resources, process raw materials into valuable products, automate production and expand your operation to increase profits. The game combines resource management, crafting and progression systems inspired by classic incremental and idle games.

---

## Quick Start

Play on your browser at:
https://click-n-mine.netlify.app/

---

## Features

### Mining

Extract ores to begin building your production chain.

Current resources include:

- Copper Ore
- Tin Ore
- Iron Ore

Gather resources by hiring miners.

### Processing

Transform raw materials into more valuable goods.

Production facilities include:

- Mines
- Furnaces
- Anvils

Ores can be smelted into ingots and forged into equipment for sale.

### Crafting

Create weapons and armour from processed metals.

Current craftable products include:

### Automation

Expand your operation through automation.

- Hire miners to gather resources automatically
- Construct additional production facilities
- Scale resource generation and manufacturing output

### Economy

Sell resources and crafted products to generate capital.

Capital can be reinvested into:

- More mines
- Furnaces for smelting ores
- Producing armour and weapons with anvils
- Future progression systems

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Gather ore.
2. Sell resources for capital.
3. Hire miners and expand production.
4. Smelt ores into ingots.
5. Craft weapons and armour.
6. Sell products for profit.
7. Reinvest earnings into automation and expansion.
